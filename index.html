What is "Pro Skills Battles"?

"Pro Skills Battles" Pro Skills Battle 2024 presented by EmpowerNextGen Ltd. is a multi-event competition with free workshops. The event focuses on enhancing students’ technical and professional competencies by engaging them in three distinct challenges. Participants will gain hands-on experience in essential skills such as internet research, data analysis, and presentation delivery. Each challenge is designed to promote practical learning, critical thinking, and creativity, helping participants prepare for real-world demands.
