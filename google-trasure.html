Rules & Marking

All participants must adhere to the following rules:

  1. Scores will be based on criteria, and average results will determine the winners.
  2. Contest time is 1 hour.
  3. Participants must not use AI tools or applications.
  4. During the contest, copying another participant‚Äôs search is not allowed.
  5. Using incognito mode will lead to disqualification.
  6. If more than 30% of a participant‚Äôs search is AI-detected, they will be disqualified.
  7. If the search result and the search URL source link do not match, the question will be marked zero (0).
  8. Before the competition, all participants must submit their phones and electronic devices at the front desk or to the room guard. No phones are allowed during the competition.
  9. Participants must follow the instructions of the room guard and event organizers.
  10. Participants must enter the room 30 minutes before the contest begins. If someone is late, it is their responsibility, and no exceptions will be made.
  11. A mock contest will be held before the main contest for practice. All rules, markings, and tips on how to maximize points will be explained. This mock contest will provide participants with an overview of the main round.

NB: Marking criteria will be based on performance, creativity, and adherence to the rules. Judges' decisions are final.

What is "Google Treasure Hunter" ?

The Google Treasure Hunter is an exciting, skill-based competition where participants are challenged to locate hidden information across the internet using advanced Google search techniques, organized by Empower NextGen Ltd. This competition encourages participants to become digital "treasure hunters," scouring the depths of online resources to uncover elusive information. Like modern-day pirates, competitors navigate a sea of websites and data, tackling tricky clues and puzzles along the way. Each round presents new challenges where quick thinking, creative search skills, and precision are key to finding the answers before others.
